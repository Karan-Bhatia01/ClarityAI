The Problem

Data analysts and small teams spend 60–70% of their time on repetitive, low-value tasks — cleaning messy datasets, writing preprocessing code, comparing models, and interpreting charts manually.

Most tools either require coding expertise or enforce rigid workflows, creating a gap between raw data and usable insights.

For non-specialists, this gap is even larger — limiting their ability to extract value from data despite having domain knowledge.

The Solution

Vera is an end-to-end platform that automates the entire data intelligence workflow — from raw CSV to production-ready insights and trained models.

Automatic summaries, quality flags, and column-level insights powered by LLM analysis.

🧹

Guided Preprocessing

📈

Automated EDA
